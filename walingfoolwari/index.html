TOURIST STANDARD GUEST HOUSE IN NEW BUS PARK KATHMANDU, NEPAL

We like to serve our guests and customers. Our aim is to make our guest happy and satisfy while they are living in our guest house. Many people from villages go to Gulf countries for job purpose daily. When they come to Kathmandu, they don't know where to live. Most of them come to New Buspark as it is the only in and out main bus station/terminal of Kathmandu Nepal. Waling Fulbari Guest House is near from New Buspark, Gongabu, Kathmandu. It is a few minutes walk from BG Mall of Gongabu on main road. The Guest House also provide pick up and drop service to the guest to the airport. So, even when the guests come from abroad, it is good for them to live here as they will get the bus to make their way to the home. The Guest House is a quiet and family environment to live and stay while you are in Kathmandu, Nepal. Waling Fulbari Guest House is Tourist standard Guest House in new bus park Kathmandu Nepal.

Waling Fulbari Guest House is an affordable hotel to live in Kathmandu. The guest house has its own building which is built recently, previously it had rented a building for the hotel, which is a few meters away from its own building now. The hotel is 6 and a half story tall and you will see the board at the gate. If you are confused about the location, you can check the map as well. The map is in our contact page, you can check there or if you google map typing Waling Fulbari Guest House, you will find it.
